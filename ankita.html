1 • Smart Traffic Automation
Graph · Segment Tree
Dijkstra · A* · KMP

Aarunya's traffic system processes live camera feeds across 500+ intersections detecting accidents and congestion. Road networks form weighted graphs where Dijkstra finds fastest emergency routes while A* handles dynamic obstacles. Segment trees aggregate traffic density for instant range queries, KMP scans license plates identifying offenders.

Aligned SDGs: SDG 11 – Sustainable Cities, SDG 9 – Innovation

Data Structures: vector<Node>, priority_queue — Algorithms: Dijkstra, Segment Tree, KMP
Camera → KMP Plates → Alerts Traffic → Segment Tree → Congestion Emergency → Graph A*/Dijkstra → Routes

2 • Water-Energy Optimization Grid
Edge List · Union-Find
Kruskal · Knapsack DP · BFS

Balances 150 reservoirs, 1200km pipes during peak demand. Kruskal constructs minimum-cost pipe networks using Union-Find. Knapsack DP optimally allocates energy across time slots. BFS validates connectivity during maintenance.

Aligned SDGs: SDG 6 – Clean Water, SDG 7 – Clean Energy

Data Structures: vector<Edge>, DP table — Algorithms: Kruskal MST, 0/1 Knapsack
Pipes → Kruskal → Optimal Network Demand → Knapsack DP → Fill Plan Maintenance → BFS → Continuity Check
