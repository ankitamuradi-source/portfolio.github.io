1 • Smart Waste Collection Management System
Graph · Priority Queue
Dijkstra · Greedy

This system optimizes municipal waste collection by modeling city roads and waste bins as a weighted graph. Each bin reports fill levels in real time, and collection vehicles must visit the most critical bins while minimizing fuel usage and time. Dijkstra’s algorithm computes shortest paths between bins, while a greedy strategy prioritizes bins nearing capacity.

Aligned SDGs: SDG 11 – Sustainable Cities, SDG 12 – Responsible Consumption

DS: adjacency list, priority_queue — Algo: Dijkstra, greedy scheduling
Bins → Graph → Dijkstra → Optimized Collection Route
