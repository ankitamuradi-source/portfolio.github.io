1 • Logistics Hub Optimization

SDG 9 – Industry, Innovation and Infrastructure
This business develops a centralized logistics hub that connects warehouses, suppliers, and delivery centers. It ensures faster and cost-effective transportation of goods using optimized routes. The system reduces fuel usage and delivery delays while improving supply-chain reliability. Revenue is generated through storage charges, delivery fees, and logistics service contracts.

Data Structure: Graph · Algorithm: Dijkstra’s Algorithm
Graphs model delivery locations and routes, while Dijkstra finds the shortest path, minimizing cost and delivery time.
