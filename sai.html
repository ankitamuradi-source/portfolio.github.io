Smart Energy Grid & Load Balancing
Solar Wind Dijkstra MST Greedy Knapsack

The Smart Energy Grid integrates renewable sources with intelligent load balancing across the city. Power plants and substations form a weighted graph, where MST algorithms minimize infrastructure cost and Dijkstra computes efficient real-time energy routing. Knapsack-based dynamic programming improves peak-hour renewable allocation, while greedy policies prioritize clean energy dispatch. This ensures reliable, low-loss, and sustainable energy distribution.

Aligned SDGs: SDG 7 – Affordable & Clean Energy, SDG 11 – Sustainable Cities, SDG 13 – Climate Action
Data Structures: adjacency list, priority_queue, arrays
Algorithms: Dijkstra, Prim/Kruskal (MST), Knapsack DP, Greedy
