1. Smart Energy Grid

The Smart Energy Grid aims to build a renewable and efficient power distribution system by integrating solar, wind, and intelligent load balancing across the city.

Contributions:
  • Increases renewable energy adoption
  • Reduces pollution and carbon emissions
  • Ensures stable and balanced power supply
  • Supports long-term sustainable city growth
Algorithms Used:
  • Dijkstra/Bellman-Ford for optimal power routing
  • Kruskal/Prim (MST) for low-cost grid design
  • Knapsack (DP) for energy-load optimization
  • Greedy algorithms for renewable energy prioritization

2. AI Healthcare Prediction Hub

The AI Healthcare Prediction Hub aims to predict disease outbreaks and support hospitals by analyzing large-scale health and demographic data.

Contributions:
  • Enables early detection of disease patterns
  • Strengthens hospital preparedness
  • Supports continuous public health monitoring
  • Reduces the spread and impact of outbreaks
Algorithms Used:
  • Decision-tree style classification for risk prediction
  • Dynamic Programming for modeling disease spread
  • Hashing for fast medical data retrieval
  • KMP/Rabin-Karp for pattern detection in health logs

3. Smart Mobility & Traffic Control Hub

This system aims to optimize city traffic, reduce congestion, and ensure safer movement using AI-driven routing and real-time updates.

Contributions:
  • Reduces congestion and overall travel time
  • Encourages sustainable and public transport usage
  • Improves route safety and transport planning
  • Enhances mobility efficiency across all city zones
Algorithms Used:
  • Dijkstra for real-time shortest path routing
  • Floyd–Warshall for full-city distance mapping
  • BFS/DFS for modeling traffic movement
  • Priority Queues for emergency routing and priority flow
  • Segment Trees for dynamic traffic density management

4. Satellite–IoT Disaster Monitoring Hub

This hub aims to monitor environmental changes and provide early disaster warnings using satellite imaging and IoT sensor networks.

Contributions:
  • Detects floods, storms, and ecological risks early
  • Accelerates response times for emergencies
  • Minimizes life and infrastructure losses
  • Supports climate-resilient development planning
Algorithms Used:
  • BFS/DFS for mapping the spread of disasters
  • Dynamic Programming for forecasting hazard intensity
  • String Matching for pattern detection in satellite images
  • Huffman Coding for efficient satellite data compression

Contributions:

10. Autonomous Port Logistics System

This system aims to automate cargo transfer from the port to warehouses and industries through robotics and intelligent route optimization.

Contributions:
  • Speeds up loading and unloading operations
  • Reduces human error and operational costs
  • Cuts emissions through efficient routing
  • Strengthens industrial and commercial supply chains
Algorithms Used:
  • MST (Kruskal/Prim) for designing optimal logistics networks
  • Dijkstra for fastest path determination
  • Knapsack (DP) for cargo loading optimization
  • Priority Queues for scheduling robotic and transport tasks
