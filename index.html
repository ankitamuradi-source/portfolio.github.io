1. Logistics Hub

What it is: A facility for transport, warehousing, cargo movement and distribution.

Income: Storage fees, transport contracts, handling charges, inventory management services.

SDG: SDG 8, SDG 9

Explanation: Enhances trade efficiency, reduces delivery time and boosts employment. Smart automation ensures smooth operations and supports city-level economic growth.

2. Eco Resort

What it is: A sustainable nature-based tourism and hospitality center.

Income: Room bookings, adventure packages, wellness spa, organic food.

SDG: SDG 11, SDG 13

Explanation: Eco resorts attract tourists while promoting environmental conservation. They use renewable resources and support local communities.

3. IT Training Center

What it is: A skill development center offering coding, AI, and cybersecurity courses.

Income: Training fees, certifications, corporate workshops.

SDG: SDG 4, SDG 8

Explanation: Builds a skilled workforce, reduces unemployment and supports digital innovation within the smart city.

4. Cold Storage

What it is: A temperature-controlled warehouse for preserving perishable goods.

Income: Storage charges, long-term contracts, distribution services.

SDG: SDG 2, SDG 9

Explanation: Prevents food spoilage, stabilizes prices, and supports farmers by extending shelf life of products.

5. Co-working Hub

What it is: Shared workspace for freelancers, startups and small companies.

Income: Desk rent, event hosting, meeting room charges.

SDG: SDG 8, SDG 9

Explanation: Encourages entrepreneurship and innovation by reducing office setup cost and enabling collaboration.

6. Retail Mall

What it is: A commercial center with shops, food courts, and entertainment.

Income: Shop rent, advertisements, events, food court sales.

SDG: SDG 8, SDG 11

Explanation: Boosts city lifestyle, creates jobs and drives local business engagement.

7. Multi-specialty Hospital

What it is: A medical center with multiple departments and emergency care.

Income: Treatment fees, labs, pharmacy revenue, insurance claims.

SDG: SDG 3

Explanation: Enhances health services, improves diagnostics and strengthens medical infrastructure in the city.

8. EV Charging Station

What it is: Charging stations for electric vehicles.

Income: Charging fees, subscription plans, EV fleet partnerships.

SDG: SDG 7, SDG 13

Explanation: Encourages green mobility, reduces pollution and supports transition to electric transportation.

9. Waterfront Tourism

What it is: Tourism activities around lakesides or riversides.

Income: Boating, dining, sightseeing packages, adventure activities.

SDG: SDG 8, SDG 11

Explanation: Enhances recreation facilities, attracts tourists and boosts local business growth.

10. E-commerce Center

What it is: A warehouse + digital marketplace supporting online retail.

Income: Commissions, delivery charges, seller subscriptions.

SDG: SDG 9, SDG 8

Explanation: Helps local businesses sell online, supports fast delivery and improves digital economy.
