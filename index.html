About

This portfolio demonstrates 40 business ideas across 4 team members. Each idea was designed considering Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) and implemented conceptually using core DAA techniques. The C++ code blocks are educational templates intended for learning and prototyping. They combine multiple algorithms (graph, DP, greedy, search) to show how real systems can be composed.
