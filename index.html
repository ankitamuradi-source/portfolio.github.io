Overview

The “Aarunya City” project explores how principles of data structures and algorithms can meaningfully influence the way a modern city grows, functions, and responds to its people.

Instead of treating DAA as purely theoretical, Aarunya City uses algorithmic thinking as a practical design tool — from optimising road connectivity with graph structures, to using searching and sorting logic for resource distribution, and applying predictive models to anticipate urban challenges. Through these approaches, Aarunya City demonstrates how a data‑driven mindset can enhance planning decisions, improve efficiency, and create a city that adapts intelligently while remaining sustainable.
