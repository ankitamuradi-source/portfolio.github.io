Aarunya — Smart City (Team Portfolio)

A data-structures & algorithms driven student project — city plan, budget, business ideas & team profiles.

Overview

Aarunya translates core DAA concepts into practical smart-city components: routing (graphs), optimization (DP/greedy), pattern discovery (KMP/RK) and hierarchical storage (trees/trie).

The project uses a hand-drawn masterplan and budget poster as input, and maps team business ideas to algorithmic approaches — making a classroom-to-city bridge.
