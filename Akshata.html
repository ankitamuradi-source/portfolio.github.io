Smart City Business Ideas – SDGs, Data Structures & Algorithms

1. Floating Micro-Farms

SDGs: SDG 2 (Zero Hunger), SDG 11 (Sustainable Cities), SDG 13 (Climate Action)

Floating micro-farms enable food cultivation on water surfaces using minimal land. They help reduce urban food shortages and transportation emissions. Crop selection is optimized based on limited floating area.

Data Structure: Array

Algorithm: 0/1 Knapsack Algorithm (Dynamic Programming)

2. XR Hologram Shops

SDGs: SDG 8 (Economic Growth), SDG 9 (Innovation), SDG 11 (Smart Cities)

XR hologram shops provide immersive virtual shopping experiences. Products are recommended based on user preferences. Efficient similarity matching improves customer engagement.

Data Structure: Vector

Algorithm: Cosine Similarity (Nearest Neighbor Search)

3. AI Sound Trails

SDGs: SDG 3 (Good Health), SDG 11 (Sustainable Communities)

AI sound trails enhance mental wellness in parks and walkways. Sound transitions are generated based on previous user behavior. Adaptive audio environments improve relaxation.

Data Structure: Matrix

Algorithm: Markov Chain Algorithm

4. Energy-Bike Café

SDGs: SDG 7 (Clean Energy), SDG 11 (Sustainable Cities), SDG 13 (Climate Action)

Energy-bike cafés generate electricity while users exercise. Generated energy is distributed efficiently between storage and usage. Power demand is handled dynamically.

Data Structure: Priority Queue

Algorithm: Greedy Scheduling Algorithm

5. Simulation Pods

SDGs: SDG 3 (Well-being), SDG 8 (Economic Growth)

Simulation pods provide immersive training and therapy sessions. User flow is simulated to manage limited pod availability. System efficiency is evaluated through event-based simulation.

Data Structure: Queue

Algorithm: Discrete Event Simulation

6. Eco-Bricks

SDGs: SDG 11 (Sustainable Cities), SDG 12 (Responsible Consumption)

Eco-bricks convert plastic waste into construction materials. Bricks are allocated based on strength requirements. This reduces waste and promotes recycling.

Data Structure: Multiset

Algorithm: Best-Fit Greedy Algorithm

7. Scent Navigation Maps

SDGs: SDG 3 (Good Health), SDG 11 (Inclusive Cities)

Scent-based navigation assists visually impaired users. Routes are optimized to minimize distance and maximize scent guidance. Safer navigation is ensured.

Data Structure: Graph

Algorithm: Dijkstra’s Shortest Path Algorithm

8. Drone Dining

SDGs: SDG 8 (Economic Growth), SDG 9 (Innovation), SDG 12 (Efficiency)

Drone dining enables fast and contactless food delivery. Delivery routes are optimized for minimum travel distance. Energy-efficient logistics are achieved.

Data Structure: Array

Algorithm: Nearest Neighbor Algorithm (TSP Heuristic)

9. Smart Sidewalks

SDGs: SDG 11 (Smart Cities), SDG 3 (Public Safety)

Smart sidewalks monitor pedestrian flow in real time. Crowd density is simulated to reduce congestion. Urban safety is improved.

Data Structure: 2D Grid

Algorithm: Cellular Automata

10. Meditation Pods

SDGs: SDG 3 (Mental Health), SDG 11 (Sustainable Cities)

Meditation pods provide private spaces for relaxation. Booking slots are optimized to avoid overlap. Maximum pod utilization is ensured.

Data Structure: List

Algorithm: Interval Scheduling Algorithm
