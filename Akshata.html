Smart City Business Ideas – SDGs, Data Structures & Algorithms

Floating Micro-Farms

SDG 2SDG 11SDG 13

Floating micro-farms enable sustainable food production in urban water bodies. They optimize land usage and reduce food transportation emissions. Crop selection is optimized based on limited floating area.

Data Structure: Array

Algorithm: 0/1 Knapsack (Dynamic Programming)

XR Hologram Shops

SDG 8SDG 9SDG 11

XR shops provide immersive shopping using holographic displays. Personalized recommendations enhance customer experience. Efficient similarity matching drives better product discovery.

Data Structure: Vectors

Algorithm: Cosine Similarity (Nearest Neighbor)

AI Sound Trails

SDG 3SDG 11

AI sound trails enhance mental well-being in public spaces. Sounds adapt dynamically based on user movement and history. Transitions between sounds are probabilistically generated.

Data Structure: Matrix

Algorithm: Markov Chain

Energy-Bike Café

SDG 7SDG 11SDG 13

Energy-bike cafés generate electricity while customers exercise. Energy distribution is optimized between storage and consumption. Demand is prioritized dynamically.

Data Structure: Priority Queue

Algorithm: Greedy Scheduling

Simulation Pods

SDG 3SDG 8

Simulation pods provide immersive learning and therapy sessions. User arrivals and departures are simulated for capacity planning. System efficiency is analyzed using event scheduling.

Data Structure: Queue

Algorithm: Discrete Event Simulation

Eco-Bricks

SDG 11SDG 12

Eco-bricks recycle plastic waste into construction materials. Bricks are allocated based on strength requirements. Waste reuse promotes sustainable construction.

Data Structure: Multiset

Algorithm: Best-Fit Greedy Algorithm

Scent Navigation Maps

SDG 3SDG 11

Scent-based navigation assists visually impaired individuals. Paths are optimized based on scent intensity and distance. Users experience safer and guided mobility.

Data Structure: Graph

Algorithm: Dijkstra’s Shortest Path

Drone Dining

SDG 8SDG 9SDG 12

Drone dining enables fast and contactless food delivery. Delivery routes are optimized to minimize distance and time. Efficient routing reduces energy usage.

Data Structure: Array

Algorithm: Nearest Neighbor (TSP Heuristic)

Smart Sidewalks

SDG 11SDG 3

Smart sidewalks monitor pedestrian movement in real time. Crowd density is simulated to prevent congestion. Urban safety and accessibility are improved.

Data Structure: 2D Grid

Algorithm: Cellular Automata

Meditation Pods

SDG 3SDG 11

Meditation pods offer private mental wellness spaces. Booking slots are optimized to avoid overlaps. Maximum utilization is achieved.
