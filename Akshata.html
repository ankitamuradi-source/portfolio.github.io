Smart City Business Ideas

Mapping Business Innovation with SDGs, Data Structures and DAA Algorithms

1. Floating Micro-Farms

SDGs: SDG 2 (Zero Hunger), SDG 11 (Sustainable Cities), SDG 13 (Climate Action)

Floating micro-farms utilize water bodies for growing crops in urban environments. They reduce dependency on land and minimize food transportation costs. Optimal crop selection ensures maximum yield within limited floating space.

Data Structure: Array

Algorithm: 0/1 Knapsack Algorithm (Dynamic Programming)

2. XR Hologram Shops

SDGs: SDG 8 (Decent Work), SDG 9 (Innovation), SDG 11 (Smart Cities)

XR hologram shops provide immersive and interactive shopping experiences. Customers receive personalized recommendations based on preferences. Similarity matching improves user satisfaction and sales efficiency.

Data Structure: Vector

Algorithm: Cosine Similarity (Nearest Neighbor Search)

3. AI Sound Trails

SDGs: SDG 3 (Good Health and Well-being), SDG 11 (Inclusive Cities)

AI sound trails create adaptive soundscapes in parks and public spaces. Sound transitions depend on user movement and previous patterns. This enhances relaxation and mental wellness.

Data Structure: Matrix

Algorithm: Markov Chain Algorithm

4. Energy-Bike Café

SDGs: SDG 7 (Clean Energy), SDG 11 (Sustainable Cities), SDG 13 (Climate Action)

Energy-bike cafés generate electricity while customers cycle. The generated energy is stored and distributed efficiently. Power demand is handled using priority-based allocation.

Data Structure: Priority Queue

Algorithm: Greedy Scheduling Algorithm

5. Simulation Pods

SDGs: SDG 3 (Well-being), SDG 8 (Economic Growth)

Simulation pods offer immersive training and therapy environments. User arrival and departure events are simulated. Efficient pod utilization is achieved through event-based modeling.

Data Structure: Queue

Algorithm: Discrete Event Simulation

6. Eco-Bricks

SDGs: SDG 11 (Sustainable Cities), SDG 12 (Responsible Consumption)

Eco-bricks convert plastic waste into durable building materials. Bricks are allocated based on strength and demand. This promotes recycling and waste reduction.

Data Structure: Multiset

Algorithm: Best-Fit Greedy Algorithm

7. Scent Navigation Maps

SDGs: SDG 3 (Good Health), SDG 11 (Accessible Cities)

Scent navigation assists visually impaired individuals. Paths are optimized based on scent intensity and distance. Shortest paths ensure safe and guided movement.

Data Structure: Graph

Algorithm: Dijkstra’s Shortest Path Algorithm

8. Drone Dining

SDGs: SDG 8 (Economic Growth), SDG 9 (Innovation), SDG 12 (Efficiency)

Drone dining enables fast and contactless food delivery. Delivery routes are optimized to reduce distance and time. This improves service efficiency and saves energy.

Data Structure: Array

Algorithm: Nearest Neighbor Algorithm (TSP Heuristic)

9. Smart Sidewalks

SDGs: SDG 11 (Smart Cities), SDG 3 (Public Safety)

Smart sidewalks monitor pedestrian density in real time. Crowd movement is simulated to prevent congestion. This enhances urban safety and accessibility.

Data Structure: 2D Grid

Algorithm: Cellular Automata

10. Meditation Pods

SDGs: SDG 3 (Mental Health), SDG 11 (Sustainable Cities)

Meditation pods provide private spaces for stress relief. Booking slots are scheduled to avoid overlap. Maximum pod utilization is achieved efficiently.

Data Structure: List

Algorithm: Interval Scheduling Algorithm
